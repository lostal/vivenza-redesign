# **App Name**: Vivenza

## Core Features:

- Hero Showcase: Full-screen hero section on the homepage with high-quality images and a concise tagline.
- Product Galleries: Interactive galleries for product categories with filtering options.
- Location Finder: Interactive map displaying exhibition locations with contact information.
- Contact Form: User-friendly contact form with data protection compliance.
- News Blog: Blog section for updates, industry insights, and design inspiration.
- Multilingual Support: Multilingual support to cater to a diverse audience.
- AI Style Finder: AI powered 'style finder' tool which suggests suitable products based on a customer-supplied photo of the space in question. Tool uses reasoning to decide when or if to incorporate information in its output.
- Apple Style: Apple style. Smooth animations, minimalist and beautiful ui/ux. Oiginal page.
- Data Source: Uses data from https://vivenzaexpo.es/

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark blue (#106984) to evoke a sense of trust and stability, reflecting the brand's reliability and the aquatic aspect of bathrooms.
- Dark gray (#222222) for a modern and sophisticated dark mode appearance.
- A lighter tone of dark blue (#4488A7) to draw attention to key interactive elements. This provides a complementary contrast to the primary blue while maintaining a cohesive color scheme.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif that provides a high-end, fashionable feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif which pairs nicely with 'Playfair' for longer blocks of text.
- Simple, line-based icons with a modern aesthetic to represent product categories and other UI elements.
- Emulate Apple's minimalist design ethos with ample white space, clean lines, and a focus on clarity, adapted for dark mode.
- Smooth, subtle animations and transitions using Framer Motion to enhance user engagement and provide a polished feel.
